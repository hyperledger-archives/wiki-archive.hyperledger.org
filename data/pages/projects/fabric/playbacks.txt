Playbacks are intended to allow contributors to present a proposal or provide progress on a particular feature, to get feedback from the community. There will be a best effort to hold these on Tuesdays at 10:00 - 11:00 EST to accommodate different geos and work week schedules. You can propose new agenda topics on the [[https://chat.hyperledger.org/channel/fabric-playbacks|#fabric-playbacks]] channel.

We ask the presenter to post any session notes or follow-up items as comments to the respective Jira items.

Please look for web conference information in the [[https://chat.hyperledger.org/channel/fabric-playbacks|#fabric-playbacks]] channel prior to meeting starting.
