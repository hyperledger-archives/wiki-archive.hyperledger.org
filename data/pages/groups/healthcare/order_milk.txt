==== Name ====

** Order milk **

==== Narrative ====

Following a preterm delivery, a newborn is transferred to the Neonatal ICU. Based on a clinical assessment, the responsible practitioner decides to place a dietary order for human milk.

A prescription is generated and sent to one of the affiliated milk banks. The healthcare provider submits relevant details from the patientâ€™s diagnostic and health history.

The milk bank manager acknowledges receiving the prescription and health records and initiates review of the request.
