==== TWG China Group Report for June 2017 ====

=== Status ===
  - Team: The team were collaborating in development, adoption cases, i18n, event organization and other tasks. 
  - Meeting: 2 meetings were held with active discussions (with LF experts). All minutes can be found on the [[ groups/twgc/meetings | wiki]].
  - Collaboration and Scenarios
    - The team has collected local adoption cases of Hyperledger techniques and shared on [[groups/twgc/team_cs | wiki]]. Those cases have been shared on more community channels like maillist and wechat public account.
  - Technical Development
    - New developers are joining, see [[groups/twgc/team_di | development page]].
    - Technical Communication: active discussions happen in the rocket chat channel/maillist. 
  - i18n and education work
    - 10s of volunteers are working on i18n. 
    - 12 design documentation are done and put on [[groups/twgc/team_ie | wiki]]. Now will do the review work and update following the newer version.
    - Education/training documentation have been collected.
  - Event organization
    - Hackfest were held on June 19-20 at Beijing, with 150+ attendees. Speeches are given on fabric, cello, identity and more, with enthusiastic discussions. The full agenda can be found at [[groups/twgc/hackfest-beijing| Hackfest-Agenda]].
    - Hyperledger technical workshop were held on June 18 in Beijing, with 100+ attendees. Topics include technical dive-in and hands-on practices.
