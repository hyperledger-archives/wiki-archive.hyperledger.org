Please feel free to use the following presentations when speaking about Hyperledger at a [[community:meetups|meetup]], internally at your organization or anywhere else where people are interested in learning more about the project.  

You are also welcome to help us improve these resources by contributing a new presentation or by translating one of these presentations into another language.  If you are interested in helping improve these presentations, post on the [[https://lists.hyperledger.org/g/discuss|Hyperledger discuss list]].

**Presentations in English**

  * [[https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1lM_FSlTDDN-5yjL3lM61hW_PIFAvGA592XN-Sv_5LrU/edit#slide=id.p4|Hyperledger Overview]] (Google Slides) -- This presentation provides information about how the Hyperledger project and community is organized

  * [[https://drive.google.com/open?id=1tgY2J9T5NHZTZr3c4t42FSE165SupYEtpNTqDgIEku4|Participating and Contributing to Hyperledger]] -- This presentation provides an overview of how to participate and contribute to Hyperledger.
  * [[https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1v618FB9b9qN_af62RB4bPfMwmyw7bWdPtwvvbKoAC8Y/edit#slide=id.g36320c4f82_0_533|Contributing to Hyperledger (For Developers)]] (Google Slides) -- This presentation provides details about how developers can get involved with Hyperledger projects.
  * [[https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1XDHuxuTHv3Y-BSag4NO3Ns_B3dxndPC--znGj3hrONE|Getting Started with Hyperledger for Developers]] (Google Slides) -- This presentation covers steps developers can take to get started coding on Hyperledger projects
  * [[https://drive.google.com/file/d/1HgAh5XhqMLfRxCTMfOXs-o90e83tl3t0/view?usp=sharing| Hyperledger Consensus Algorithms]] (Google Slides) -- This presentation covers consensus fundamentals, the different Hyperledger projects and their algorithms, and getting involved with Hyperledger
  * [[https://github.com/yeasy/seminar-talk/tree/master/hyperledger-tech-notes| Hyperledger Tech Notes]] - Several slides related to Hyperledger technologies.
